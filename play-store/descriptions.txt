FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
==========================================
Little Dino is a friendly AI chatbot that helps children discover age-appropriate books and videos tailored to their interests, reading level, and age.

Kids simply chat with Little Dino -- a cheerful dinosaur buddy -- and receive personalised recommendations for books to read and videos to watch. Every recommendation is filtered for safety and age-appropriateness.

KEY FEATURES

Chat with Little Dino
Ask for book or video suggestions in a fun, conversational way. Little Dino understands what your child enjoys and recommends content they will love.

Smart Recommendations
Powered by AI, recommendations adapt to your child's interests, reading level, and favourites over time. The more they use it, the better it gets.

Built-in Book Reader & Video Player
Read books and watch videos directly in the app through a safe, kid-friendly viewer. No need to leave the app.

Favourites
Children can save the books and videos they love and revisit them anytime from their personal favourites collection.

Personal Library
Browse a curated library of books and videos suited to your child's profile, with personalised top picks.

PARENT FEATURES

Parent Dashboard
Monitor your child's reading activity, favourite content, and screen time from a dedicated parent view.

Screen Time Controls
Set daily screen time limits with built-in warnings so children develop healthy digital habits.

Content Filters
Control the maximum age rating and block specific topics to ensure your child only sees content you approve.

Parental PIN & Email Verification
Secure parent accounts with email verification and protect settings with a parental PIN.

Invite Codes
Easily link your child's account to your parent account using a simple invite code system.

SAFETY FIRST

Every aspect of Little Dino is designed with child safety in mind:
- Age-appropriate content filtering
- Safe in-app browsing with URL validation
- No ads or in-app purchases that target children
- Screen time management built in
- Parent-controlled content approval options

Little Dino makes reading fun and screen time meaningful. Download now and let your child's next adventure begin!
