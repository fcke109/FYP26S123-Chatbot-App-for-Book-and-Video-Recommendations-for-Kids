About

About the Little Dino project

Little Dino is a chatbot-based platform designed to help children discover books and educational videos in a safer and more guided way.

This website connects to the same Firebase backend as the Android app, letting you browse the book catalog and manage your account right from the browser.

Designed for families

Little Dino combines child-friendly design, guided recommendations, and future-ready parent features to create a more reassuring digital experience.

  • Chatbot-based discovery
  • Books and videos in one place
  • Safer, guided exploration
  • Firebase-connected web experience
