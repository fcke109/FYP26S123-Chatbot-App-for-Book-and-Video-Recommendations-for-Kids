Privacy Policy

How Little Dino handles data and privacy

Last updated: April 2026. This privacy policy explains what information Little Dino collects, how it is used, and how families can manage their data.

1. Introduction

Little Dino ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our app and website. This policy applies to the Little Dino Android app and the website at fyp-chatbot-81a50.web.app.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, age, and account type (child or parent).
  • Profile preferences: Interests, reading level, and content filters.
  • Usage data: Chat messages with the chatbot, reading history, favourite books and videos, and screen time data.

We do not collect location data, device identifiers, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalisation: To provide age-appropriate book and video recommendations.
  • Chatbot responses: Chat messages are sent to our AI service to generate relevant, safe responses.
  • Parental monitoring: To allow parents to view their child's activity.
  • Service improvement: To improve the quality and safety of recommendations.

4. Children's Privacy

Little Dino is designed for children aged 3-12. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Child accounts are linked to a parent or guardian account.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent children's personal data to third parties.
  • All content is filtered for age-appropriateness and safety.
  • Parents can view and manage all data associated with their child's account.
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, data storage, and hosting.
  • Google Gemini AI: To power the chatbot. Chat messages are processed but not used to train AI models.
  • YouTube API: To provide safe video recommendations with strict content filtering.
  • Open Library / Internet Archive: To provide free book content.

6. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is controlled by role-based security rules. Parent accounts are protected with email verification and an optional parental PIN.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Account data is retained while the account is active. Parents can request deletion of their child's account and all associated data. Upon deletion, all personal data, chat history, favourites, and reading history are permanently removed.

8. Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information and activity.
  • Update or correct their child's information.
  • Request deletion of their child's account and data.
  • Set content filters and screen time limits.
  • Approve or reject content recommendations.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: littledino075@gmail.com
